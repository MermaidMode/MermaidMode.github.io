MEET THE ARTIST

Victoria Carolina is a New York based 3D Generalist, Illustrator, and Designer. She is currently completing an Animation and Motion Graphics A.S. Degree at Borough of Manhattan Community College. In the Fall 2018 semester she will continue her studies in Computer Art, Computer Animation, and Special Effects at the renowned School of Visual Arts in New York City. Victoria currently holds a B.F.A. in Art History from Hunter College, giving her a superb visual vocabulary and sense of design.

Victoria lives in Brooklyn with her amazing senior citizen Yorkie named Tina and her husband. She spends her time playing with as many dogs as she can find, reading all the fantasy book series, creating art, and escaping into nature for a nice long hike.
